Objectives

The society has been created in 2017 to provide a common platform for communication, collaboration and sharing of knowledgebase among researchers working in the area of materials, biology and allied disciplines.

It will be involved in promoting interdisciplinary research in the fields of materials science and biology and bring together academicians with different expertise to facilitate collaboration and intergroup mobility. The society will also be involved in organization and participation of people in conferences, workshops, symposia and discussion meetings for exchange of ideas in the field of materials and biology and related areas of research.

One of the highlights will also be to undertake developmental and outreach activities in the field by reaching out to school kids and providing scholarship and travel support for bright and intelligent students to attend national and international conferences/meetings in related field.
